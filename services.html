Services

Everything from your first dashboard to a full data platform.

Pick a single project or lean on us as an ongoing analytics partner — every engagement starts with a clear, fixed scope.

Business Intelligence & Dashboards

Power BI and Tableau dashboards designed around the handful of metrics that actually drive decisions — not a wall of charts nobody opens.

  • Executive & operational dashboards
  • KPI design & data storytelling
  • Self-serve reporting for teams

Data Engineering & ETL

Pipelines and warehouses that keep your data clean, current, and ready to report on — built on Snowflake, SQL Server, and modern cloud platforms.

  • ETL / ELT pipeline development
  • Snowflake & cloud data warehousing
  • PySpark for large-scale processing

AI & Machine Learning

Models built around one clear business question — forecasting demand, predicting churn, or flagging fraud — with a plan for how they'll actually be used.

  • Forecasting & demand planning
  • Customer churn prediction
  • Fraud & anomaly detection

Cloud Migration & Analytics

Moving reporting and data workloads onto Azure or AWS without disrupting the teams who depend on them day to day.

  • Cloud data migration planning
  • Azure & AWS analytics setup
  • Cost & performance tuning

Analytics Consulting

An outside, senior-level read on your current data stack — what's working, what's fragile, and where to invest next.

  • Data stack & maturity assessment
  • Roadmap & tool selection
  • SQL & query performance review

Corporate Training

Hands-on training for your team in Power BI, Tableau, SQL, or PySpark, so good reporting doesn't depend on outside help.

  • Power BI & Tableau workshops
  • SQL fundamentals for analysts
  • Custom, team-specific curricula
